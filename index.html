About the Temple

The temple is famous for its unique tradition of worship and the delicious 'prasadam' served to the devotees. The temple complex includes several other shrines and a beautiful garden.The Udupi Krishna Temple is a renowned Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, located in Udupi, Karnataka. Known for its rich history and spiritual significance, the temple is famous for its unique worship practices and the deity's idol, which is considered to be self-manifested. Visitors come to the temple to offer prayers, participate in religious ceremonies, and experience its peaceful atmosphere. The temple also plays a central role in local cultural and religious events, drawing devotees from all over for its vibrant celebrations and spiritual teachings.
